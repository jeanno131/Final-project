About Me

Nike, Inc. is an American multinational corporation engaged in design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel, equipment, accessories, and services. The company is headquartered near Beaverton, Oregon, in the Portland metropolitan area. It is the world's largest supplier of athletic shoes and apparel and a major manufacturer of sports equipment.

Our Journey

Founded by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight on January 25, 1964, as "Blue Ribbon Sports", the company officially became Nike, Inc. on May 30, 1971. The company takes its name from Nike, the Greek goddess of victory. Nike markets its products under its own brand, as well as Nike Golf, Nike Pro, Nike+, Air Jordan, Nike Blazers, Air Force 1, Nike Dunk, Foamposite, Nike Skateboarding, and subsidiaries including Brand Jordan, Hurley International and Converse.

Nike has been a global leader in sportswear innovation, constantly pushing boundaries in athletic performance and style. Our commitment to athletes and innovation drives everything we do.

What Makes Us Unique

  • Innovation & Technology: Pioneering advancements like Nike Air, Flyknit, and React foam to enhance athletic performance.
  • Iconic Branding: The "Swoosh" and "Just Do It" slogan are globally recognized symbols of athletic excellence.
  • Athlete Endorsements: Collaborations with top athletes worldwide, reinforcing our credibility and aspirational image.
  • Global Reach & Influence: A dominant presence across continents.
  • Commitment to Sustainability: Initiatives like "Move to Zero" focusing on reducing environmental impact.
